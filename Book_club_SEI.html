Book Club and Social Emotional Intelligence

Course Objective

  • To inculcate a habit of book reading among the students, for improving their vocabulary, comprehension, critical thinking and reflecting on the perspectives provided by various authors on varying titles.
  • To make the students self-aware and manage their emotions, develop social awareness and social skills. The course should help the student's build and maintain healthy relationships through establishing a safe, positive, and mutually beneficial environment, both at the workplace and in their personal lives.
  • Developing appreciation for International literature and culture, reflect upon the ideas provided by the authors, and to articulate their own reflections on the topics.


    • As a part of Social Emotional Intelligence, students should be able to:

    • Develop Self-Awareness
    • Manage their emotions
    • Take Responsible Decisions
    • Develop Social Awareness
    • Build Relationship Skills

    Syllabus

  • At least two books to be read by every student and analyzed as a debrief in the class, facilitated by a professor.
  • The Conch Bearer - Chitra Banerjee Divakaruni
  • The Alchemist - Paulo Coelho

    • We have covered

    1. Full book: The Alchemist
    2. 12 Chapters of The Conch Bearer
    3. Concept of Belongingness
